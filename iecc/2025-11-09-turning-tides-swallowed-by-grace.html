üé§ Main Sermon Content

God called Jonah to go to Nineveh, the capital of Assyria, to preach repentance. But understanding why Jonah refused requires understanding just how evil Nineveh was. This wasn't fear‚Äîit was hatred.

üî• The Assyrian Empire's Brutality

In the 8th century BC, Assyria was known for unimaginable violence: impaling captives, flaying skin to display on city walls, stacking heads outside conquered cities. King Ashur-Nasirpal II declared: "I am the merciless weapon that strikes down the land of his enemies."

A modern comparison: it would be like asking a Jewish person during WWII to go to Berlin and preach repentance to Hitler. That's the magnitude of what God asked Jonah to do.
