🙏 Jehovah Rapha ✨

The Lord Who Heals

📖 Based on Exodus 15:22-26

💧 "If you listen carefully to the Lord your God and do what is right in his eyes, if you pay attention to his commands and keep all his decrees, I will not bring on you any of the diseases I brought on the Egyptians, for I am the Lord who heals you."

- Exodus 15:26

1

🍎 Sin Leads to Bitterness

⚠️ Original sin brought curses into the world:

🐍 Conflict between humans and nature

💔 Broken relationships and painful childbirth

🌪️ Nature produces thorns and thistles

💀 Death entered the world

Genesis 3:14-19
Romans 8:22
🎯 Application:

Our suffering isn't God's fault - it's the result of living in a fallen world. This gives us hope because God has a plan to reverse these curses!

2

🌈 Complete Healing Will Come

🎉 God will reverse ALL the curses of the fall!

🏠 No more death - people will live as long as trees

🌾 Nature will cooperate perfectly

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Perfect relationships restored

🦁🐑 Lion and lamb will feed together

Isaiah 65:17-25
Revelation 21:4
🎯 Application:

Your current pain has an expiration date! Hold onto hope - God's ultimate healing plan includes everyone who trusts in Jesus.

3

👁️ Some Get a Glimpse of Glory

✨ God still heals today as a foretaste of the future!

🦽➡️🚶 Physical healing can happen

⚰️➡️💝 Jesus raised 3 people from the dead

🩸➡️🙌 Woman bleeding 12 years was healed

🔮 These are previews of the coming kingdom

Mark 5:25-34
John 11:43-44
🎯 Application:

If you've experienced healing, treasure it and share your story! If not, don't lose hope - your complete healing is guaranteed in the future.

4

⚖️ Healing & Faith Don't Always Correlate

🚫 It's NOT about praying hard enough!

🚫 It's NOT about having enough faith!

🚫 It's NOT because you've done something wrong!

✝️ The New Covenant is based on faith in Jesus, not perfect behavior

🤗 Jesus promises to be our constant companion in suffering

2 Corinthians 12:7-10
Hebrews 13:5
🎯 Application:

Stop blaming yourself for unanswered prayers. Instead, look for Jesus' presence with you in your pain. His companionship is the greatest gift of all.

💝 God's Promise to You

🤝 "My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness." - 2 Corinthians 12:9


🌟 Whether you experience healing now or wait for the ultimate healing in eternity, know that Jesus walks with you every step of the way. You are never alone in your pain.
