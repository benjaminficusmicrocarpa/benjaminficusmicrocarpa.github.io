üß• Randy's Letter Jacket: Don't Live in Yesterday

üçΩÔ∏è Biblical Fasting: Missing the Point

Just to be clear: breakfast is to break fast. I fast every night while I sleep. So committed. And then I eat breakfast. The very word in English is to break a fast.

Biblical fasting is NOT a Christian diet plan. Oh, how many times have we seen that? "The Christian loves to fast and get spiritual kudos as well as lose weight. Yes, please." Not the point.

The whole point of fasting was to have intentional deprivation of food for spiritual intimacy so you can encounter God. Jesus says, "You boys have lost the point. My disciples, how could they be any closer to God? I am God. While I'm here, they don't need to fast to sense God's presence."
