üåç The Whole Creation Groans

"We know that the whole creation has been groaning as in the pains of childbirth right up to the present time." - Romans 8:22

Every wilting flower, every dying tree, every destructive storm is creation groaning under the bondage of death.

Ancient cultures understood there was a connection between sin and sickness. Even Jesus acknowledged this when he told the healed man at Bethesda: "See, you are well again. Stop sinning or something worse may happen to you."

Modern medicine has separated physical sickness from sin entirely. But while your specific sickness may not be related to your personal sin, it is certainly connected to original sin - the corruption that entered when death and decay invaded God's perfect creation.

Important: This doesn't mean your illness is punishment for something you've done. It means we live in a fallen world where death and disease exist because of humanity's initial rebellion against God.

Just as God purified the bitter waters at Marah by having Moses throw in a piece of wood, God promises to reverse all the curses of the fall. This is the great hope of scripture - complete restoration is coming.

Old Covenant (Exodus 15:26):

"If you listen carefully... if you do what is right... if you pay attention... if you keep all his decrees, I will not bring on you any of the diseases..."

Four conditional "if" statements - essentially requiring perfect obedience to hundreds of rules and regulations.

New Covenant:

Much simpler - just have faith in Jesus Christ. Faith that Jesus died for your sins and rose from the grave. This is the promise given to all who confess faith in Jesus.

The Israelites failed to keep the old covenant (see the golden calf in Exodus 32, just chapters later). The new covenant isn't based on our performance but on Jesus' perfect performance for us.

üîç Jesus' Healing Ministry in Perspective

Jesus healed many people, but for every one person he healed, thousands he didn't heal. For every one person he raised from the dead (three total), thousands remained in the grave.

But those who experienced his healing got a precious foretaste, a glimpse of the future where there is no more pain and destruction.

Lazarus (raised from dead):

Got to experience a glimpse of a world without death - though he would eventually die again, he tasted the resurrection power that awaits all believers.

Woman with 12-year bleeding:

If you experience healing today, treasure it and share the story. You've been given a gracious gift of experiencing what the future restoration will be like for everyone who belongs to Jesus.
