Modern Worldview Classifications

In modern society, worldviews are better understood by their view of reality:

Reality is physical‚Äîwhat you see is real. Includes materialism, secularism, hedonism.

2. Which opposing worldview is most difficult for you to counter with your biblical understanding?

In other words, what non-biblical influences are you around the most?

Practical step: Identify one area where you're most influenced by non-biblical thinking. Commit to studying what Scripture says about that topic this week.

3. What "normal things" have you made into idols?

‚ñº

God's prohibition in Eden was about eating‚Äîa normal, neutral, necessary activity. He was showing that sin isn't just about "bad" things, but about making anything more important than God.

  • Relationships and approval from others
  • Comfort, entertainment, or leisure
  • Health, fitness, or appearance
  • Your children's success or your family's reputation
