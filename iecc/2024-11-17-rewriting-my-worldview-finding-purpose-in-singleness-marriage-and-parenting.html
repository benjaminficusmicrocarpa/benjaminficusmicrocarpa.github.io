üíï The Weather Channel Love Story:

Early Dating: "We would talk all night on the phone. Literally all night. Back then we didn't have the internet, so I would watch the Weather Channel just to see when sunrise was. I'd be like, 'okay, sunrise is at 5:45, so we're going to talk until 5:45' just to prove we stayed up all night talking."

This illustrates the difference between "being in love" (the explosion) and "love" (the engine that runs the marriage).

üîÑ Love vs. Being in Love:

Being in love: The euphoric feeling, the explosion that started it all - when you can't think straight, write their name on everything.
Love: The engine that runs the marriage - a habit strengthened by grace, choosing to love even when you don't like each other, just as you love yourself even when you don't like yourself.

üí™ Why Parenting is Hard:

Notice the word "train" in Proverbs 22:6. It's not just hope that a child will grow in the way they should go. It actually takes effort. It takes work. Why is parenting so hard? Because you're trying so hard. You're training. You're working. It's nonstop. It's every day.

Parents as Home Pastors:

  • Training - Active, intentional effort (not hoping)
  • Discipling - Children become your primary disciples at home
  • Modeling - "They're not going to listen to what you say, they're going to listen to what you do"
  • Teaching Diligently - In house, walking, lying down, rising up
  • Releasing - Eventually launching them into their own mission
