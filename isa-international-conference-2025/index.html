üèõÔ∏è About ISA Hong Kong Chapter

The International Society of Arboriculture (ISA) Hong Kong Chapter is dedicated to advancing the practice of arboriculture and urban forestry in Hong Kong and the broader Asia-Pacific region. As part of the global ISA network, the Hong Kong Chapter promotes professional development, research, and education in tree care and management.

The chapter serves as a platform for arborists, urban foresters, researchers, and tree care professionals to share knowledge, exchange best practices, and collaborate on initiatives that enhance urban forest health and sustainability. Through conferences, workshops, and educational programs, the chapter contributes to the advancement of arboricultural science and practice.

The 2025 International Conference represents the chapter's commitment to fostering international collaboration and knowledge exchange in urban forestry and arboriculture, addressing the unique challenges and opportunities in rapidly urbanizing Asian cities.
