About the Hong Kong Botanica 關於香港草木誌

An independent botanical archive documenting the vascular flora of one of Asia's most biodiverse urban territories. 一個獨立的植物學檔案庫，記錄亞洲最具生物多樣性的城市地區之一的維管束植物。

Our Mission我們的使命
"A personal, independent initiative to meticulously catalog, photograph, and document the diverse vascular plants found across Hong Kong — sitting at the heart of the Indo-Burma biodiversity hotspot." 「一個獨立的個人項目，旨在仔細分類、拍攝和記錄遍佈香港的各種維管束植物 —— 地處印度—緬甸生物多樣性熱點的核心地帶。」

This database serves as both a personal field notebook and an open reference, combining firsthand observations with established taxonomic frameworks — freely available to naturalists, educators, and anyone curious about Hong Kong's remarkable plant life. 本數據庫既是個人的野外筆記，也是公開的參考資料，結合了第一手觀察與已建立的分類學框架——免費提供給自然愛好者、教育工作者以及任何對香港豐富植物多樣性感興趣的人。

Biogeographic Context生物地理背景

The Indo-Burma Biodiversity Hotspot 印度—緬甸生物多樣性熱點

Hong Kong occupies a unique biogeographic position at the southeastern edge of the Indo-Burma biodiversity hotspot, one of 36 global hotspots defined by Conservation International. This region stretches from eastern India through Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam, and into southern China — encompassing an extraordinary concentration of endemic and threatened species. 香港位於印度—緬甸生物多樣性熱點的東南邊緣，佔據了獨特的生物地理位置。該區域是國際保育組織定義的全球36個生物多樣性熱點之一，從印度東部延伸經緬甸、泰國、越南至中國南方——匯聚了大量特有和受威脅物種。

Despite a total land area of only approximately 1,110 km², Hong Kong hosts over 3,000 vascular plant species — a density of floral diversity that far exceeds expectations for a territory of its size. This richness arises from the convergence of subtropical monsoon climate, varied geology (granite, volcanic, sedimentary), and topographic complexity ranging from sea-level mangroves to the 957-metre summit of Tai Mo Shan. 儘管香港的總面積僅約1,110 平方公里，卻擁有超過3,000種維管束植物——植物多樣性密度遠超同等面積地區的預期。這種豐富性源於亞熱帶季風氣候、多樣的地質條件（花崗岩、火山岩、沉積岩）以及從海平面紅樹林到大帽山957米山頂的複雜地形的交匯。

The territory's position at the Tropic of Cancer, combined with maritime influences from the South China Sea, creates a mosaic of microhabitats — fung shui woodland, montane cloud forest, freshwater marsh, coastal strand — each supporting distinct plant communities. 香港位處北回歸線，加上南海海洋的影響，形成了多元的微生境——風水林、山地雲霧林、淡水沼澤、海岸灘地——各自支撐著獨特的植物群落。

Attribution & Acknowledgement 引用與致謝

This project builds upon decades of dedicated botanical research in Hong Kong. The Hong Kong Botanica would not be possible without the foundational work of the researchers, institutions, and field botanists who have collectively documented the territory's remarkable flora. 本項目建基於數十年來香港植物學研究的累積成果。若非研究人員、機構及野外植物學家的奠基性工作，香港草木誌不可能實現。他們共同記錄了本地區豐富的植物多樣性。

Taxonomy in this database strictly follows the Angiosperm Phylogeny Group IV (APG IV, 2016) classification system, ensuring alignment with current phylogenetic understanding. 本數據庫的分類學嚴格遵循被子植物系統發育組第四版（APG IV，2016）分類系統，確保與最新的系統發育學認知一致。
