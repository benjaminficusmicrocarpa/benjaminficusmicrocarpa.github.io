What is the Hong Kong Botanica? 什麼是香港草木誌？

This archive is a personal, independent initiative to meticulously catalog, photograph, and document the diverse vascular plants found across Hong Kong's complex topography—from coastal mangroves to high-altitude montane forests. 本檔案庫是一個獨立的個人項目，旨在仔細分類、拍攝和記錄遍佈香港複雜地形（從沿海紅樹林到高海拔山地森林）的各種維管束植物。

Despite its small landmass, Hong Kong sits squarely within the Indo-Burma biodiversity hotspot, hosting an astonishing variety of flora. This database serves as both a field notebook and an open reference, combining personal field observations with established taxonomic frameworks. 儘管面積不大，香港正處於印緬生物多樣性熱點，擁有令人驚嘆的植物多樣性。本數據庫既是野外筆記，也是公開的參考資料，結合了個人的野外觀察與已建立的分類學框架。

Data Sources & Taxonomy數據來源與分類學

This database synthesizes personal observations with authoritative botanical literature and modern taxonomic frameworks. 本數據庫綜合了個人觀察、權威植物學文獻及現代分類學框架。

Field Observations野外觀察

Photographic records, phenology data, and habitat notes collected directly from trails, country parks, and urban spaces across Hong Kong. 直接從香港的行山徑、郊野公園及市區空間收集的攝影紀錄、物候數據及生境筆記。

Regional Literature地區文獻

Cross-referenced with the Flora of Hong Kong (AFCD), checklists by the HK Herbarium, and ecological data from Kadoorie Farm and Botanic Garden (KFBG). 與漁護署的《香港植物誌》、香港植物標本室的名錄，以及嘉道理農場暨植物園的生態數據進行交叉比對。

Global Taxonomy全球分類學

Nomenclature and family assignments strictly follow the APG IV system, verified against Plants of the World Online (POWO) and WFO. 命名和科級分類嚴格遵循 APG IV 系統，並與世界在線植物誌 (POWO) 及 WFO 進行核對。
