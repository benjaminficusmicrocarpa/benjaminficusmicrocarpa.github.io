Seasonal Phenology Calendar 四季物候日曆

A year-round overview of Hong Kong's flowering patterns, shaped by the subtropical monsoon climate. 香港全年開花規律概覽，受亞熱帶季風氣候影響。

Spring春季

Mar – May · 三月至五月 三月至五月 · Mar – May

Peak diversity. Hillsides ablaze with rhododendrons and kapok. Bauhinias in full bloom across urban parks. 物種多樣性高峰。山坡上杜鵑花和木棉怒放，紫荊花在城市公園中盛開。

Rhododendron spp.
Bauhinia blakeana
Bombax ceiba

Summer夏季

Jun – Aug · 六月至八月 六月至八月 · Jun – Aug

Tropical species peak during monsoon rains. Lotus ponds are at their finest; crape myrtles line the streets. 熱帶物種在季風雨季達到高峰。蓮花池最為壯觀，紫薇花開滿街道。

Nelumbo nucifera
Lagerstroemia spp.
Delonix regia

Autumn秋季

Sep – Nov · 九月至十一月 九月至十一月 · Sep – Nov

Late bloomers and fragrant species. Sweet osmanthus fills the air; chrysanthemums add autumn colour. 晚開花及芬芳物種。桂花香氣四溢，菊花增添秋色。

Osmanthus fragrans
Chrysanthemum spp.
Liriope spp.

Winter冬季

Dec – Feb · 十二月至二月 十二月至二月 · Dec – Feb

Camellias emerge in the cool season. Bauhinia blakeana continues its extended bloom into the new year. 山茶花在涼季盛開，洋紫荊延續至新年仍繼續綻放。

Camellia spp.
Bauhinia blakeana
Prunus spp.
