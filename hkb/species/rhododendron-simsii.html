Rhododendron simsii

Red Azalea 映山紅 · 紅杜鵑

Planch.

Home首頁 Species Directory物種目錄 Rhododendron simsii

Taxonomic Classification 分類學分類

Kingdom界 Plantae
Division門 Magnoliophyta
Class綱 Magnoliopsida
Order目 Ericales
Family科 Ericaceae
Genus屬 Rhododendron
Species種 R. simsii

Plant morphology 植物形態

General Habit 一般習性

Growth Form生長形態

Ascending shrub, usually evergreen, 1–2(–3) m tall, much-branched.

斜昇灌木，通常常綠，高 1–2(–3) 米，多分枝。

Bark & Branches

Branchlets densely strigose with stiff appressed light brown triangular flat scale-like hairs.

小枝密被堅硬貼伏的淺褐色三角形扁平鱗片狀糙伏毛。

Special Notes備註

Probably the most common wild rhododendron in S China and one of the most widely cultivated in E Asia.

可能是華南地區最常見的野生杜鵑，在東亞亦被廣泛栽培。

Leaves 葉

Shape形狀

Elliptic to obovate, alternate, spirally arranged; margin entire and flat, base cuneate, apex acute, obtuse to round with a blunt point.

橢圓形至倒卵形，互生，螺旋狀排列；全緣且平展，基部楔形，先端急尖、鈍或圓形並具鈍尖頭。

Size大小

Petiole 1.0–3.0 mm; leaf blade 1.5–5.5 cm long, 1.0–2.2 cm wide.

葉柄 1.0–3.0 毫米；葉片長 1.5–5.5 厘米，闊 1.0–2.2 厘米。

Texture & Color

Thick papery; strigose on both surfaces, often becoming glabrous adaxially, but retaining hairs abaxially.

厚紙質；兩面具糙伏毛，葉面常變無毛，葉底常保留毛茸。

Special Notes備註

Often crowded towards nodes of branches.

常密集生於枝條節處。

Flowers 花

Appearance外觀

Inflorescence 1–2(–3)-flowered; calyx 5-lobed; corolla funnel-shaped, 3.5–4.5 cm in diam., with elliptic to obovate lobes.

花序具 1–2(–3) 朵花；花萼 5 裂；花冠漏斗狀，直徑 3.5–4.5 厘米，裂片橢圓形至倒卵形。

Color顏色

Bright red, with dark red dots on posterior lobes.

鮮紅色，後部裂片具深紅色斑點。

Stamens & Ovary

10 stamens (sparsely pubescent below middle); ovary ovoid, 5-locular, densely strigose.

雄蕊 10 枚（中下部疏生柔毛）；子房卵球形，5 室，密被糙伏毛。

Flowering Period花期

December to May.

12 月至翌年 5 月。

Special Notes備註

Flower buds borne in axils at stem apex, covered in strigose hairs.

花芽生於莖頂端葉腋內，外部被糙伏毛。

Fruits 果

Type & Characteristics

Capsule, ovoid to rarely ellipsoid, strigose; seeds many, elliptic.

蒴果，卵球形至罕見橢球形，具糙伏毛；種子多數，橢圓形。

Size大小

Capsule 7–9 × 3–5 mm; seeds 1.2–1.5 × 0.3–0.4 mm.

蒴果 7–9 × 3–5 毫米；種子 1.2–1.5 × 0.3–0.4 毫米。

Fruiting Period果期

October to December.

10 月至 12 月。

Ecology 生態學

  • Habitat & Elevation: Thrives in degraded, fire-prone environments and acidic soils at elevations of 500–2700 m.
  • Fire Adaptation: Demonstrates rapid post-fire regeneration, typically flowering within 18 months following a burn event.
  • Pollination: Obligate insect-pollinated (facultatively outcrossing) plant; primary pollinators include Apis cerana, Bombus eximius, and Xylocopa spp.
  • Reproductive Metrics: Daily nectar production averages 0.9–38.7 uL per flower; produces 2–135 seeds per flower with individual seed weights of 0.11–0.45 mg.
  • Germination: Seeds require direct light exposure for successful germination, leading to near-zero seedling recruitment under shaded canopies.
  • Thermal Constraints: Exhibits low heat stress tolerance; exposure to 50 C for 30 minutes yields a 31.7%–38.9% cellular relative injury rate, indicating vulnerability to extreme warming.

Landscape Architecture 景觀設計

  • Aesthetic Configuration: Maximize visual quality (Scenic Beauty Estimation) by aggregating high-saturation red floral patches (R: 220–255, G: 0–60, B: 0–50).
  • Patch Metrics: Optimize the landscape shape index and separation index by avoiding geometric over-complexity and extreme patch dispersion to improve visual coherence.
  • Visual Contrast: Increase the ratio of red patch area to black patch area to elevate chromatic contrast against shaded or bare ground.
  • Physical Dimensions: Deploy as a mid-story shrub layer; mature specimens reach 2 m (rarely up to 5 m) in height with canopy widths averaging 1.53 +/- 0.25 m.
  • Flowering Period: Plan for peak floral displays between late February and May.

Urban Forestry 城市林業

  • Air Quality Improvement: Utilize as a highly effective bio-filter for capturing inhalable atmospheric particulate matter (PM).
  • Particulate Retention Rates: Retains 11.04 +/- 0.47 g/m2 of Total Suspended Particulates, 6.80 +/- 0.52 g/m2 of PM10, and 1.39 +/- 0.10 g/m2 of PM2.5 per unit leaf area.
  • Microstructural Mechanisms: High PM capture efficiency is driven by stomatal density (19.88 +/- 1.89 mm-2) and stomatal area (966.87 +/- 32.24 mm2), which positively correlate with PM deposition.
  • Pollutant Remediation: Effectively combats volatile organic compounds like formaldehyde emitted from urban building materials.
  • Planting Recommendations: Prioritize deployment alongside traffic corridors and highly polluted zones to leverage its superior PM10 retention capacity.

Phytosociology 植群生態學

  • Community Structure: Forms dense upland thickets and understory layers within Pinus massoniana forests and secondary tropical/subtropical successions.
  • Canopy Dynamics: Experiences significant negative growth and flowering impacts from dense shading; optimal performance requires semi-open to fully open canopy structures.
  • Sympatric Associations: Frequently co-occurs with pioneer species such as Rhododendron farrerae in exposed, highly disturbed microsites.
  • Biomass Allocation: Exerts intense localized competitive pressure; low sub-canopy light levels drastically suppress understory herbaceous species and restrict its own seedling recruitment.
  • Conservation Status: Although regionally abundant, wild populations are often legally protected; damaging wild plants on government land in Hong Kong incurs maximum penalties of $25,000 fines and one-year imprisonment.

Ethnobotany 民族植物學

  • Toxicity: Leaves and flowers contain grayanotoxins, which are highly poisonous to humans and foraging mammals.
  • Intoxication Risks: Consumption of contaminated "mad honey" or direct ingestion of plant parts leads to severe gastrointestinal, cardiac, and central nervous system poisoning.
  • Phytochemical Yields: Yields biologically active secondary metabolites, including specific triterpenes and flavanone glycosides.
  • Cultural Significance: Widely cultivated ornamentally in Chinese gardens and historically associated with good luck and prosperity in Feng Shui traditions.
  • Indoor Application: Cultivars (Belgian hybrids/Florist's Azaleas) are propagated commercially for potted display and indoor aesthetic enhancement.
